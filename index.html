Who We Are

Built By Players,
For Players

Gateway Smash Pickleball was created in response to high fees, inconsistent play, and disorganized management found in many corporate leagues. We're a grassroots, volunteer-run community focused on fairness, affordability, and fun, without sacrificing competitive structure. All matches are held indoors at Arch Pickleball to ensure reliable play, rain or shine. By using free tools like WhatsApp, Notion, and Global Pickleball Network, we keep costs low and operations simple. Every dollar collected goes directly toward court rental. Whether you're new to the game or an experienced player, Gateway Smash offers a welcoming, well-run alternative for pickleball in St. Louis.

Inclusive & Player-First

Gateway Smash is a grassroots, volunteer-run league built for all skill levels. Whether you're brand new to the game or chasing a 4.0 rating, you'll find a welcoming and supportive environment here.

Structured Leagues & Competitive Play

Our seasonal indoor ladder leagues offer consistent, rain-or-shine match play with weekly updates using the Elo ranking system. You'll always be matched with players near your skill level for fair and fun competition.

Community & Connection

We use tools like WhatsApp and Global Pickleball Network to foster connection off the court. From substitute coordination to post-match banter, Gateway Smash makes it easy to get involved and stay connected.
