Welcome to Gateway Smash Pickleball

Gateway Smash Pickleball is a grassroots, volunteer-run pickleball community in St. Louis offering affordable leagues for all skill levels. We’re committed to making pickleball inclusive, fun, and fitness focused no matter your background or experience.

Join Us Today

About Gateway Smash

Gateway Smash Pickleball was created in response to high fees, inconsistent play, and disorganized management found in many corporate leagues. We’re a grassroots, volunteer-run community focused on fairness, affordability, and fun, without sacrificing competitive structure. All matches are held indoors at Arch Pickleball to ensure reliable play, rain or shine. By using free tools like GroupMe, Notion, and Global Pickleball Network, we keep costs low and operations simple. Every dollar collected goes directly toward court rental. Whether you’re new to the game or an experienced player, Gateway Smash offers a welcoming, well-run alternative for pickleball in St. Louis.
